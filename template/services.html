Construction Security Services

After regular business hours, construction sites are deserted until the next work day. Expensive machinery and equipment are often left unguarded, leaving the site open to potential theft or vandalism. When materials and equipment are damaged or stolen, projects are delayed for an indefinite period of time, and unforeseen additional expenses can potentially arise, cutting into profit margins. GN Security has built its construction security services expertise for construction sites. Our team combines extensive experience with the industry’s best practices to protect our client’s people and assets. Comprehensive construction security services include:

  • Construction site surveillance
  • Risk assessment
  • Loss Prevention
  • Mobile Patrols
